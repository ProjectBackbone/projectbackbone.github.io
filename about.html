Kavish Senthilkumar | Founder

        Currently a student at Conestoga High School, Kavish founded Project Backbone to help fellow students gain a better understanding of the body and its mechanisms. He actively enjoys learning about anatomy and physiology, and this passion culminated in his competing in the Anatomy event at the Science Olympiad national competition. In his free time, Kavish works in a lab studying Alzhiemer's disease tissues and additionally works in bioinformatics drawing conclusions frow sequence data.
