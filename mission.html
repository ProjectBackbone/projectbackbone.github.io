Mission

Project Backbone's Goals

Who We Are

At Project Backbone we believe that educating the next-generation of K-12 students in health and research can both help them gain a better understanding of the medical principles around them




What We Do

With a multi-tiered approach, we teach students about concepts in research. In addition to working with basic body systems, we work to focus on presenting disease symptoms, causes, and treatment. Additionally, we will take a look at fields like genetics, computational biology, and other data-driven methods. Project Backbone aims to provide an interactive learning environment and concerns itself with two main goals - educate and experiment.
