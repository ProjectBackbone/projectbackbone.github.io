Mission

Project Backbone's Goals

Who We Are

At Project Backbone we believe that educating the next-generation of K-12 students in health and bodily systems can both help them prevent disease and gain a better understanding of the medical principles around them




What We Do

With a multi-tiered approach, we teach students about concepts in anatomy and physiology. In addition to working with basic body systems, we work to focus on presenting disease symptoms, causes, and treatment. By first presenting an illness on a symptom-based level, then analyzing specific causes and risk factors, we provide an engaging, interactive learning environment. In addition, we try to review case studies for real-world exposure and take feedback for a better program overall.
