Project Backbone

Teaching what makes us tick: explaining the human body to students everywhere

At Project Backbone, we aim to educate others about the inner workings of the human body. With a focus on disease, our goal is for complete understanding of the how and the why behind mechanisms we take for granted.

Mission

Ischaemic heart disease is the leading cause of death in the world ( World Health Organization, 2017 ). But what exactly is it? How can you avoid it? It is questions like these that Project Backbone wants to help answer - questions that seek to know more about a condition than its name and symptoms.
